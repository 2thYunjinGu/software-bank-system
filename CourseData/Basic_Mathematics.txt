Course Title: Basic Mathematics

Module 1: Introduction to Numbers
- Understand and use the concepts of numbers.
- Differentiate between various types of numbers like natural numbers, whole numbers, integers, rational numbers, and irrational numbers.

Module 2: Basic Operations
- Learn the four basic mathematical operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
- Practice these operations with positive and negative numbers.

Module 3: Fractions and Decimals
- Understand the concept of fractions and decimals.
- Convert fractions to decimals and vice versa.
- Solve problems involving fractions and decimals.

Module 4: Introduction to Algebra
- Understand the use of variables in mathematics.
- Learn simple algebraic expressions and equations.
- Solve simple equations.

This course aims to provide a solid foundation in basic mathematical concepts and operations, preparing students for more advanced topics in mathematics.
